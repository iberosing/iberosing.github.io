Accomodation

It is important to take into account that Granada has a high tourist activity, and even more especially during the weekends. Therefore, you can generally expect high occupancy rates. We recommend booking your accommodations as soon as possible and well in advance. Here are some recommendations:

  • Hotel Catalonia
  • Luna Granada
  • Granada Center
  • Hotel Reina Cristina
  • Hotel Don Juan
  • Pensión San Joaquín
