Registration

Please, fill out the following official registration form:

https://eventos.upm.es/go/iberosing24

There is two types of registration:

  • Registration fee: 80€.
  • Reduced registration fee: 40€. This includes young researchers (Master/PhD students).

Registration deadline: 17/11/24.

Poster submission: Participants willing to present a poster (or being selected to give a lightning talk) should send a plain *.tex file (use "name_surname.tex" as syntax) with the corresponding title and abstract to iberosing (-at-) ucm (-dot-) es. Submission deadline: 17/11/24.

 

If you have any comment or doubt, please send an email to iberosing (-at-) ucm (-dot-) es.
