How to arrive

The conference will take place at the Retiro Building of the ETSI Caminos, Canales y Puertos (Univ. Politécnica de Madrid). Note that the location is not placed in the Moncloa Campus, but in the Retiro Park instead.

C. de Alfonso XII, 3, y 5, Retiro, 28014 Madrid

The Retiro Building is close to Atocha Station and the Downtown. The easiest way to reach the conference is by metro (Line 1), commuter train (Cercanías) or bus.
