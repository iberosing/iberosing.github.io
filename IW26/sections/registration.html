Registration Instructions

Please complete the Zaragoza form if you plan to attend the first week, and complete the Andorra form if you plan to attend the second week. Attending both parts of IberoSing 2026 requires completing both registration processes.

Poster and Lightning Talk Submission

Instructions for submitting poster or talk proposals are provided in the corresponding registration process for each event.

  • Zaragoza: after registering, participants should send the *.tex files by email, as indicated on the Zaragoza registration page. Lightning talks will be selected from these submissions.
  • Andorra: the registration form asks participants to upload a *.tex file with their proposed talk or poster.
