About the Workshop

Welcome to the fifth edition of the IberoSing International Workshop, organized by the Iberoamerican Webinar of Young Researchers in Singularity Theory. This year's event spans two exciting locations, offering a unique blend of educational content and cutting-edge research.

The workshop aims to bring together young and senior researchers in Singularity Theory and related areas, providing an enriching environment for learning and collaboration. This is a face-to-face event.
